
# ONLINE DOT IN SERVICE

O.IS (Online Dot In Service) is a unified digital service platform designed to simplify, automate, and streamline public service applications and related offline services through a single ecosystem.

The platform focuses on reducing manual effort, eliminating repetitive form filling, minimizing errors, and improving accessibility for students, job seekers, and citizens‚Äîespecially in regions where service centers are costly or limited.


## Vision

To build a trusted digital ecosystem where users can access essential services easily, affordably, and efficiently‚Äîwithout long queues, repeated data entry, or dependency on physical service centers.
